About Us

Welcome to Happy Bee's Cafe!

We offer a cosy area to eat in or can prepare your order for takeaway. Telephone orders are welcome and we also offer a local delivery service. Come and dine with us as our friendly staff are happy to serve that perfect breakfast or lunch! We are a family run business that have been serving Halifax and beyond 7 days a week for more than three years. Why not check out our Menu, Gallery, and Order pages? We're sure you'll love eating with us - but don't just take our word for it! Check out our reviews on Restaurant Guru.
